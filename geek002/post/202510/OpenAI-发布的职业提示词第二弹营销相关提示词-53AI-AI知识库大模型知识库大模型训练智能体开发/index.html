昨天我整理了一些面向管理者的提示词，今天这一批是面对市场营销相关岗位的。同样中英文对照，可以用在其他大模型中。

更多可能对工作有帮助的提示词，可以在下方的“合集”中找到。

ChatGPT 用于营销

当今的营销人员既是策略家，也是故事讲述者和分析师。他们关注共鸣的品牌叙事、可衡量的渠道影响，以及保持领先于不断变化的渠道——同时不牺牲真实性或包容性。

ChatGPT 帮助他们构思活动角度，更快地迭代文案，挖掘受众洞察，并测试信息传递效果，以便他们能够以市场的速度交付高质量的资产。

活动策划与策略

ChatGPT 支持构建、组织和构思营销活动。使用推理模型进行战略性头脑风暴。

可视化活动时间线

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为我们的即将到来的多渠道活动制定时间表。关键日期和里程碑是：[插入信息]。以水平时间轴形式输出，包含阶段、负责人和截止日期。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Build a timeline for our upcoming multi-channel campaign. Key dates and milestones are: [insert info]. Output as a horizontal timeline with phases, owners, and deadlines.</p></section>  
</section>

头脑风暴活动创意

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为即将到来的[活动/发布]头脑风暴 5 个创意活动点子。目标受众是[插入目标]，我们的目标是[插入目标]。每个点子包含一个主题、一句口号和 1-2 个核心策略。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Brainstorm 5 creative campaign ideas for our upcoming [event/launch]. The audience is [insert target], and our goal is [insert goal]. Include a theme, tagline, and 1-2 core tactics per idea.</p></section>  
</section>

起草创意简报

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为我们的下一次付费媒体活动创建一个创意简报。以下是目标、受众和优惠信息：[插入信息]。包括目标、受众洞察、语气、所需资产和 KPI 等部分。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Create a creative brief for our next paid media campaign. Here's the goal, audience, and offer: [insert info]. Include sections for objective, audience insights, tone, assets needed, and KPIs.</p></section>  
</section>

构建信息框架

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为新产品构建信息框架。产品详细信息为：[插入信息]。输出一个包含三个支柱的表格：关键优势、证明点和情感触发因素。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Build a messaging framework for a new product. The product details are: [insert info]. Output a table with 3 pillars: key benefits, proof points, and emotional triggers.</p></section>  
</section>

构建客户旅程地图

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为我们的[产品/服务]创建一个客户旅程地图。我们的典型客户是[插入画像]。将其分为阶段、目标、触点和每个阶段的潜在痛点。以表格形式输出。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Create a customer journey map for our [product/service]. Our typical customer is [insert profile]. Break it into stages, goals, touchpoints, and potential pain points per stage. Output as a table.</p></section>  
</section>

竞争和市场研究

ChatGPT 可以支持进行竞争对手分析、基准测试和新兴趋势的研究。使用网络搜索或深度研究以获得更深入的实时洞察。

竞争内容分析

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>研究前五名竞争对手如何构建他们的博客内容策略。包括语气、主题、频率、SEO 重点和 CTA。提供 URL、要点，以及一个总结常见和突出策略的表格。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Research how top 5 competitors structure their blog content strategy. Include tone, topics, frequency, SEO focus, and CTAs. Provide URLs, takeaways, and a table summarizing common and standout tactics.</p></section>  
</section>

研究买方行为的新兴趋势

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>研究 2024 年[type]买方如何研究和评估[行业]产品的新趋势。包括行为变化、内容偏好和渠道使用。引用来源，并以简报形式呈现，要点用项目符号标注。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Research 2024 trends in how [type] buyers research and evaluate [industry] products. Include behavior shifts, content preferences, and channel usage. Cite sources and format as a short briefing with bullet-point insights.</p></section>  
</section>

研究区域活动基准

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>研究 2024 年针对[地点]的数字广告活动中的典型点击率、每次点击成本和转化率。重点关注[广告渠道]。包含来源链接，并附上按国家比较各项指标的表格。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Research typical CTRs, CPCs, and conversion rates for digital campaigns targeting [location] in 2024. Focus on [ad channels]. Include source links and a table comparing each metric by country.</p></section>  
</section>

研究行业活动竞争对手的参与情况

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>整理竞争对手参与[插入即将到来的活动]的总结。包括展位活动、演讲环节、赞助和媒体覆盖。以包含链接和分析的表格形式输出。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Compile a summary of how our competitors are participating in [insert upcoming event]. Include booth activations, speaking sessions, sponsorships, and media coverage. Output as a table with links and analysis.</p></section>  
</section>

研究营销人员使用的 AI 工具

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>研究按功能（例如文案撰写、规划、分析、设计）最受推荐的营销工具。创建一个包含功能、价格、优缺点和主要应用场景的表格。附上来源。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Research the most recommended [tools] for marketers by function (e.g. copywriting, planning, analytics, design). Create a table with features, pricing, pros/cons, and primary use case. Include sources.</p></section>  
</section>

内容与创意开发

ChatGPT 可以协助你生成营销文案、视觉素材和资产。使用 canvas 进行实时编辑。

起草产品发布邮件

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>撰写一封关于我们新产品的发布邮件。使用以下关于产品和目标受众的信息：[插入详细信息]。使其具有吸引力和说服力，格式化为一份准备审查的营销邮件。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Write a launch email for our new product. Use the following info about the product and target audience: [insert details]. Make it engaging and persuasive, formatted as a marketing email ready for review.</p></section>  
</section>

生成广告文案变体

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为[渠道]活动创建 5 个广告文案变体。以下是活动主题和受众信息：[插入背景]。每个版本应测试不同的钩子或语气。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Create 5 ad copy variations for a [channel] campaign. Here’s the campaign theme and audience info: [insert context]. Each version should test a different hook or tone.</p></section>  
</section>

创建社交帖子系列

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>撰写一个推广[活动、产品或里程碑]的三篇社交媒体系列文案。使用以下背景信息作为语境：[粘贴详细信息]。每篇帖子应包含文案和一个建议的视觉描述。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Draft a 3-post social media series promoting [event, product, or milestone]. Use this background for context: [paste details]. Each post should include copy and a suggested visual description.</p></section>  
</section>

创建一个客户聚焦帖子

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>根据以下成功案例的关键细节：[粘贴详细信息]，撰写一篇客户聚焦帖子。使其对话式、真实，并与我们的品牌声音保持一致。输出为 LinkedIn 帖子草稿。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Write a customer spotlight post based on this success story: [paste key details]. Make it conversational, authentic, and aligned to our brand voice. Output as a LinkedIn post draft.</p></section>  
</section>

<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为[产品/主题]撰写一个 60 秒的解说视频脚本。以下是它应涵盖的内容：[插入信息]。使其简洁明了，并建议视觉或动画效果。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Draft a script for a 60-second explainer video about [product/topic]. Here’s what it should cover: [insert info]. Make it punchy and clear, with suggested visuals or animations.</p></section>  
</section>

数据分析与优化

ChatGPT 可以分析数据、预测趋势并改进决策。上传数据以进行更深入的分析。

识别表现最佳的营销渠道

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>分析这份营销绩效电子表格，并识别出 ROI 最高的渠道。该文件包含从 Q1 到 Q2 期间电子邮件、社交、付费搜索和活动的营销活动数据。总结前三个渠道，并创建一个按渠道展示 ROI 的图表。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Analyze this marketing performance spreadsheet and identify which channels had the highest ROI. The file includes data from Q1–Q2 campaigns across email, social, paid search, and events. Summarize top 3 channels and create a chart showing ROI by channel.</p></section>  
</section>

揭示客户流失模式

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>审查这个客户流失数据集，并识别流失客户的共同特征。使用诸如服务年限、产品使用情况和支持工单等列来分组见解。输出一个简短的总结，并附有图表或表格显示主要风险因素。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Review this customer churn dataset and identify common characteristics of churned customers. Use columns like tenure, product usage, and support tickets to group insights. Output a short summary with a chart or table showing top risk factors.</p></section>  
</section>

总结调查结果

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>总结此次活动后客户反馈调查的见解。文件包含满意度评分和开放式回复。提供 3 点要点式执行摘要，并制作一张显示主要满意度驱动因素的图表。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Summarize insights from this post-campaign customer feedback survey. The file includes satisfaction ratings and open-ended responses. Provide a 3-bullet executive summary and a chart of top satisfaction drivers.</p></section>  
</section>

预测下一季度的潜在客户数量

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>使用过去 6 个季度的历史潜在客户数量数据，预测下一季度的预期潜在客户数量。突出显示任何趋势、季节性模式，并输出一个简单的预测图表。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Use this historical lead volume data from the past 6 quarters to project expected lead volume for the next quarter. Highlight any trends, seasonal patterns, and output a simple forecast chart.</p></section>  
</section>

优化活动预算分配

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>根据这份以往活动支出和回报的电子表格，为下一季度推荐修改后的预算分配方案。重点在于最大化投资回报率，同时减少对表现不佳渠道的支出。以表格形式输出新的百分比分配。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Based on this spreadsheet of previous campaign spend and returns, recommend a revised budget allocation for next quarter. Focus on maximizing ROI while reducing spend on underperforming channels. Output as a table with new % allocations.</p></section>  
</section>

视觉与品牌沟通

专注于制定连贯的视觉策略、品牌叙事和创意概念。使用 ChatGPT 创建图像。

制定品牌风格指南大纲

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为[公司/产品]创建品牌风格指南大纲，包括字体、色彩搭配、标志使用、语气风格、图像风格以及应做与不应做的事项。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Create an outline for a brand style guide for [company/product]. Include sections for typography, color palette, logo usage, tone of voice, imagery style, and do’s/don’ts.</p></section>  
</section>

构思视觉叙事

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>为[主题]的品牌活动构思 3 个视觉叙事概念，包括概念名称、视觉风格和关键叙事元素（例如，故事弧线、氛围、色彩）。</p></section>  
</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Brainstorm 3 visual storytelling concepts for a brand campaign on [theme]. Include a concept name, visual style, and key narrative elements (e.g., story arc, mood, colors).</p></section>  
</section>

创建视觉活动情绪板

<section>1</section>

</section>  

  
<section>  
<section>1</section>  
<section><p>Create a moodboard with 4 visuals for our [campaign or brand update]. Theme is [describe theme], and the tone should be [describe tone]. Use photoreal or illustrated style.</p></section>  
</section>
