vocabulary

[vəˈkæbjələri] 词汇，词汇量

释义

1. 词汇量；词汇表：the range of language of a particular person, group, or subject.
2. 词汇表：a list of words with their meanings, especially in a book for learning a foreign language.

例句

You need to improve your vocabulary if you want to pass the exam.

如果你想通过考试，需要提高你的词汇量。

The book has a vocabulary at the back.

这本书的后面有词汇表。

词根记忆

vocab(词汇) + ul + ary(表名词，"有...性质的") → vocabulary(词汇)
